Design and test a function `ss3: number(a) number(b) -> number` to sum
the squares of every third number from `a` to `b`. Skip multiples of
ten.

    (ss3 14 23) = 14^2 + 17^2 + 23^2
    (ss3 14 18) = 14^2 + 17^2
    (ss3 10 40) = 6165
