## fibsum

`(fibsum n)`: Sum of Fibonacci numbers 0 through n.

| n | (fibsum n) | simplified |
|---|------------|------------|
| 0 |     1      | 1 |
| 1 |  1+1    | 2 |
| 2 | 1+1+2   | 4 |
| 3 | 1+1+2+3 | 7 |
| 4 | 1+1+2+3+5 | 12 |
| 5 | 1+1+2+3+5+8 | 20 |
