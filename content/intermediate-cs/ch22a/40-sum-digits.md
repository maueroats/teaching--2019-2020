`sum-digits`: whole-number -> number

Find the sum of the digits in a number.

| n |`(sum-digits n)` | simplified |
|---|-------------------|-----------|
| 5 | 5 | 5 |
| 53 | 5 + 3 | 8 |
| 921 | 9 + 2 + 1 | 12 |
| 3216 | 3 + 2 + 1 + 6 | 12 |
| 65536 | 6 + 5 + 5 + 3 + 6 | 25 |
